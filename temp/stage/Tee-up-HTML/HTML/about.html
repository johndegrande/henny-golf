History

The Monte-Carlo Golf Club designed by Willie Park, Jr. opened in 1911. It offers an 18-hole golf course in an idyllic setting between the sea and the mountains! The golf course, located in La Turbie (in the heights of Nice), has a rolling terrain that requires extreme precision. Discover an elegant club in a warm and friendly ambience!
